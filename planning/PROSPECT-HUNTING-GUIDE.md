## The Target: "Alex Morgan"

### Demographics
- **Age:** 35-55
- **Role:** Founder, CEO, Managing Partner, Owner, Principal
- **Company type:** Consultancy, advisory firm, professional services, B2B agency
- **Company size:** 5-50 employees
- **Revenue:** â‚¬500k-â‚¬5M (estimated based on team size)
- **Location:** US, UK, Canada, Australia, Western Europe (English-speaking)

### Psychographics
- Knows personal branding drives business
- Has expertise worth sharing
- Time-poor (running the business takes priority)
- Hates filming or being on camera
- Not tech-averse, but not a "creator"
- Values premium, done-for-you solutions
- Can afford $6-12k for the right solution

### Behavioral Signals (Look for these)
- LinkedIn profile exists but posts infrequently (< 2x/month)
- No video content at all (or very little)
- Has posted thought leadership before (shows they understand value)
- Engages with others' content (active but not posting)
- Company page exists but is quiet
- Has written articles/blogs in past (knows content matters)

### Red Flags (Skip these)
- Already posting video regularly
- Has a large creator following (10k+)
- Too small (solopreneur, just starting)
- Too large (100+ employees, would go enterprise route)
- In industries we can't serve (healthcare, finance with compliance issues)

## LinkedIn Search Strategy

### Basic Search (No Sales Navigator)

**Search 1: Consulting Founders**
```
Title: "Founder" OR "CEO" OR "Managing Partner"
Current Company: "consulting" OR "advisory" OR "consultancy"
Location: United States
```

**Search 2: Agency Owners**
```
Title: "Founder" OR "Owner" OR "CEO"
Current Company: "agency" 
Industry: Marketing, Business Consulting
Location: United Kingdom, Canada, Australia
```

**Search 3: Professional Services**
```
Title: "Principal" OR "Managing Director" OR "Partner"
Industry: Management Consulting, Professional Services
Location: All English-speaking
```

### Advanced Search (With Sales Navigator)

**Ideal Filters:**
- Title: Founder, CEO, Owner, Managing Partner, Principal
- Industry: Management Consulting, Professional Services, Business Consulting
- Company headcount: 2-50
- Years in current role: 2+ (established, not just started)
- Posted on LinkedIn: In last 30 days (active account)
- Geography: US, UK, Canada, Australia

**Boolean Search:**
```
(Founder OR CEO OR "Managing Partner" OR Owner) 
AND (consulting OR advisory OR consultancy OR "professional services")
NOT (recruiting OR staffing OR "real estate")
```
