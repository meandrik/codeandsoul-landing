### üéØ Track 2: Prospect Hunting

**Day 3-7: Build the List**

**Search Criteria (LinkedIn):**
- Title: Founder, CEO, Managing Director, Partner, Owner
- Industry: Management Consulting, Professional Services, Business Consulting
- Company size: 2-50 employees
- Location: US, UK, Canada, Australia (English-speaking)
- Activity: Posted in last 30 days
- Red flag indicator: No video content, infrequent posting

**List Structure:**
```
| Name | Company | LinkedIn URL | Pain Indicator | Notes |
|------|---------|--------------|----------------|-------|
| ... | ... | ... | "Last post 2 months ago" | ... |
```

**Target: 100 names by Day 7**

**Day 7-21: Outreach Execution**

**The Warm Outreach Framework:**

```
Stage 1: CONNECT (Day 1)
- Send personalized connection request
- Note: Reference something specific about them
- Example: "Hi [Name], loved your post about [topic]. 
  Building something in the AI content space ‚Äî would love to connect."

Stage 2: VALUE (Days 2-5)
- Like/comment on their content (genuine)
- Share something useful without asking for anything
- Build familiarity before pitching

Stage 3: CURIOSITY (Day 5-7)
- Soft mention of what we do
- Example: "Been working on AI avatar systems for founders 
  who want to post video without filming. Your content 
  on [topic] made me think you might find it interesting."

Stage 4: OFFER (Day 7-10)
- Offer free diagnostic call
- No pitch, no pressure
- Example: "Would you be open to a 15-min chat? 
  Happy to share what we've learned ‚Äî no strings attached."
```

**Volume Targets:**
- Connection requests: 10/day
- Active conversations: 5-10 at any time
- Diagnostic calls: 2-3/week

## Risk Mitigation

### Scenario: Content Not Gaining Traction (Day 14)
**Response:**
- Increase outreach volume (15/day instead of 10)
- Test different content angles
- Consider paid LinkedIn InMails ($30 each)
- Tap personal networks for warm intros

### Scenario: No Qualified Leads (Day 21)
**Response:**
- Revisit ICP definition ‚Äî too narrow?
- Test adjacent markets (coaches, agencies)
- Offer "founding client" discount (20% off)
- Consider pilot offer (smaller scope, lower price)

### Scenario: Proposals Not Converting (Day 25)
**Response:**
- Urgency play ("Only 2 spots in February")
- Payment plan option
- Scope adjustment (smaller first project)
- Direct ask: "What would it take?"
